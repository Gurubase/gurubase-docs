## What is Gurubase?

Gurubase is an [open-source (Apache 2.0) RAG system](https://github.com/Gurubase/gurubase) that lets you create AI-powered Q&A assistants ("Gurus") for any topic or need. Create a new Guru by adding:
- 📄 Webpages
- 📑 PDFs
- 🎥 YouTube videos
- 💻 GitHub repositories

Start asking questions directly on Gurubase, or embed it on your website to let your users ask questions about your product.
