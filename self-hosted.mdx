## Overview

Gurubase Self-Hosted Enterprise Edition (Onprem / On-Premise) allows you to deploy and run Gurubase entirely within your own infrastructure. Whether you need an onprem deployment for security reasons or compliance requirements, our self-hosted onprem solution gives you complete control over your data, security, and deployment configuration.
